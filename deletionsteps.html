Account Deletion Instructions
Request to Delete Your Account and Associated Data

We value your privacy and are committed to providing transparency and control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data for our app, you can submit a request using the button below.

Please note:

  • This request will permanently delete your account and all related data stored by us.
  • The data that will be deleted includes your profile, preferences, usage history, and any content you've uploaded.
  • We do not retain any personally identifiable information after the account is deleted.
  • Some non-personal or anonymized data may be retained for analytics or legal purposes, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

To request account deletion, please complete the form linked below. We'll process your request within 7 business days.

Request Account Deletion
Step 1: Open the RoomsVital App
Launch the RoomsVital mobile application on your device and make sure you're logged in with the account you wish to delete.

Step 2: Go to Profile or Settings
Tap on the profile icon located at the top-right (or bottom menu) to open the profile or account settings section.

Step 3: Select 'Account Settings'
Within the profile section, find and tap on ‘Account Settings’.

Step 4: Tap on 'Delete My Account'
Scroll to the bottom of Account Settings and select the option ‘Delete My Account’.

Step 5: Confirm Deletion
A warning prompt will appear stating that this action is permanent. Confirm the deletion by entering your password (if required) and tapping ‘Confirm’.

Step 6: Final Notification
You will see a confirmation message. Your account and all associated data will be scheduled for deletion and will be removed from our servers within 7 working days.

Important Notes:
- Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.
- Make sure to back up any important information before deleting.
- If you're facing issues, please email us directly.
